Go Greenish is an student run environmental non-profit focused on establishing gradual change in local communities to support a larger sustainability movement. This is happening by canvassing to receive sustainability promises from general people as well as helping local businesses transition to a more sustainable future. We really appreciate any donation, big or small, and these will help our organization become more established.
Where your funds go:
  • Website Maintanence
  • 501(c)(3) expenses
  • Starting events funds (beach cleanups, yard sales, canvassing local parks)
