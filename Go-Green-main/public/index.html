Our Mission and Vision

Go Greenish is an environmental 501(c)(3) statewide activist movement working to bridge student empowerment and environmentalism through advocacy and youth-led policy. We envision a world where the youth voice is involved in climate policy and innovation given that climate change is an intergenerational problem that inherently impacts youth more than adults.

WHAT WE DO

Promises

Our promise system initiates gradual sustainable change to empower individuals and communities, change the status quo, and build consistent habits through outreach and education.

Policy

Our current policy introduces sustainability legislation to transition San Dieguito Unified High School District to zero carbon emissions by 2050 through a district committee.

Outreach

Our outreach is through publishing digestible information through social media and being involved in the San Diego climate activist community.

Newsletter

We publicize digestible content monthly on the most relevant topics surrounding climate change and the future of our planet.

Volunteering

Go Greenish has virtual and in-person volunteering events very often so anyone and everyone can get involved and do their part!

Curriculum

We create curriculum to expand our movement and spread our message
