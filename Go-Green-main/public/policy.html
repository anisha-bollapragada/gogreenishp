OUR WORK

Fighting the intergenerational gap in climate implementation

Climate change is an intergenerational problem. Children are more affected by climate change than their parents or grandparents. Go Greenish was founded on the basis that in order to mitigate and adapt to climate change holistically, the approach must be taken with the inclusion of students and schools. Go Greenish works to replace rhetoric from adults saying that we need to listen to youth. Rather, we emphasize that we need to involve youth in climate policy and implementation in order to address climate change with urgency and effectiveness.

Promises

Our promise system initiates gradual sustainable change to empower individuals and communities, change the status quo, and build consistent habits through outreach and education.

Policy

Our current policy introduces sustainability legislation to transition San Dieguito Unified High School District to zero carbon emissions by 2050 through a district committee.
