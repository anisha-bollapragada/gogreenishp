Our Story

Climate change is a prominent threat in today’s society, and one matter is certain: time is limited. Mindsets such as “Do it tomorrow” or “someone else can fix it” are the main reasons why people overlook and ignore this issue. Relying on others to fix this problem is impractical. Which is why we, ourselves, must take action.

The Go Greenish campaign was created on the basis of knowing that we have the ability to make an impact and difference in the world we live in. There is a hopeful future that all starts with one promise. By joining the Greenish community, you take the first step in actively making change in the environment around you. We will act to change the minds of many and shed light on the effects of climate change.
